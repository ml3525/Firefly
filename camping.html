  • Access to showers, restrooms, food vendors, live music performances from Firefly artists, late night silent disco, yoga and more.
  • Access to Ranger Stations offering information, restrooms, water, charging, waste drop off and more.

WHAT TO PACK

  • Tent
  • Pillow, sleeping bag and sleeping pad
  • Camping Gear
  • Shelter, tarp or tapestry for shade
  • Camping chairs and tables
  • Reusable water bottles (refill stations available)
  • Cooler Sunscreen and bug repellent
  • Headlamp or flashlight
  • Lantern
  • Sunglasses
  • Rain Gear
  • Towel
  • Hat
  • Bandana
  • Jacket or hoodie
  • Extra sneakers / boots
  • Toiletries (toothpaste, deodorant, etc)
  • Band-aids and first aid kit
  • A clear backpack
  • Biodegradable plastic bags for trash (we provide some bags for trash and recycling, but extras are nice!)
  • Extra set of keys to keep with you at all times
  • Photo ID
  • Flag / balloon / other way to identify your campsite
